<h4>- Description</h4>
<p class="building-description">
Warren houses approximately 100 upperclass students in double rooms. Each room has a private bath and is divided by a wardrobe into semi-private living areas. This privacy wall is not shown in the floor plans.
<br />
The first floor of Warren has a well-equipped kitchen and lounge.
<br />
A printer is located on the first floor. The printer room has an outside entrance and is accessible to all campus residents.
</p>
