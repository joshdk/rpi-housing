<h4>- Description</h4>
<p class="building-description">
North Hall is conveniently located near the main campus on Sage Avenue and houses 87 upper-class students. Nearly half of the rooms are singles, and the rest of the rooms are doubles.

Each floor has two baths that are shared by approximately 22 students and a kitchen. There is a large lounge on the main floor for study, reading, socializing, or television viewing.
</p>
